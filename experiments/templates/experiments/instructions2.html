General Information

At the beginning of the experiment you will be randomly divided into groups of 6 members each. During the whole experiment, you will interact only within your group. At the beginning of the experiment every participant receives an endowment of 40 EMUs (experimental money units) that will be later translated into real currency (euros) with a rate of 2 to 1.

During the game, each member of the group will be able to invest the money received as an endowment in a climate account through several rounds.

The climate accounts assigned to each group have a minimum investment objective of 120 EMUs. This means that if the accumulated contributions to the account after the experiment end, are equal or higher than 120 EMUs, then each member of the group will be able to keep the amount of the endowment that they didn’t invest.

However, if this minimum is not reached, the computer will “throw a dice” so that all group members will lose all their saving with a certain probability p that will be communicated to you at the beginning of the experiment.
